Our Mission

We don't just aim to clean your clothes, but to provide you with an experience. Our mission is to provide Oceanside with a clean, efficient, and affordable option when it comes to laundry. This is just our mission, our story is much more fascinating, and it can be read below!

Our Story

We bought this wonderful laundromat and flipped it completely. We put much love and effort into repacing the machines and redoing the interior and cleaning the place up. Everyday I make sure to be present for our customers, answering their questions and meeting their needs. I make sure to make this laundromat a comfortable space for everyone. We have a toy section for the children and a little arcade game so that you can pass your time while waiting. We aso have folding areas where we use clean tables so that you can fold your clothes knowing this laundromat is a good place. We also make this laundromat comfortable by keeping affordable prices, we welcome you all!
